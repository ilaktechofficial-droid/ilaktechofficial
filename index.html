How ILAKTECH WORKS?

  • Step 1: Understanding Requirements
    ✓ We understand employer needs and candidate career goals to ensure the right match.
  • Step 2: Sourcing & Screening
    ✓ We source candidates through reliable channels and conduct initial screening, shortlisting, and verification to ensure quality and suitability.
  • Step 3: Training & Development
    ✓ For candidates who require enhancement, we provide practical training and skill development aligned with industry and employer requirements.
  • Step 4: Interview Coordination
    ✓ We coordinate interviews between employers and candidates, provide interview guidance, and support the selection process.
  • Step 5: Placement & Onboarding
    ✓ Once selected, we assist with placement formalities and ensure smooth onboarding for both employer and candidate.
  • Step 6: Post-Joining Support
    ✓ We offer follow-up and support after joining to ensure satisfaction, retention, and long-term success.
