Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)



1. What is ILAK TECH?
ILAK TECH is an HR and technology services company providing recruitment, staffing, training, and placement solutions with a focus on innovation and inclusion.
2. What services does ILAK TECH offer?
We offer recruitment & staffing, HR consulting, payroll support, training & placement assistance, and BPO/KPO manpower solutions.
3. Who can use ILAK TECH services?
Our services are available for employers, corporates, startups, institutions, job seekers, freshers, and experienced professionals.
4. Do you provide training with placement support?
Yes. We provide industry-aligned training along with placement assistance to help candidates become job-ready and secure suitable opportunities.
5. Is placement guaranteed after training?
We offer 100% placement assistance. Final placement depends on candidate performance, eligibility, and employer requirements.
6. Which industries do you serve?
We serve IT & Technology, BPO/KPO, Banking & Finance, Healthcare, Manufacturing, Retail, Sales, and Education sectors.
7. Do you support freshers and career switchers?
Yes. We actively support freshers, entry-level candidates, and career switchers through training and guidance.
8. How can employers partner with ILAK TECH?
Employers can contact us through the website, submit a requirement, or connect with our team by contact us to discuss hiring needs.
9. Do you provide post-placement support?
Yes. We provide post-placement follow-up to ensure smooth onboarding and long-term success for both candidates and employers.
10. Is ILAK TECH available for 24/7 support?
Yes. We offer common 24/7 support for employers, clients, and candidates.
